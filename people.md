## Principal Investigator

**Dr. Cheng-Fan “Bryan” Lee (李丞釩)**  
Assistant Professor  
Department of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology  
Taipei Medical University  

Dr. Lee’s research integrates cancer metabolism, lipid biology, and transcriptional remodeling to investigate mechanisms underlying prostate cancer progression and therapy resistance.

**Research Interests**
- Prostate cancer biology and therapy resistance  
- Cancer metabolism and lipid signaling  
- Transcriptional and epigenetic regulation
